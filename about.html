About Us

Welcome to Whim.


We are thrilled to introduce you to our innovative secret-sharing application designed to keep your private messages secure and confidential., a platform where your secrets are safe with us. In today's digital age, privacy is more important.

How it Works:
  1. Write your message: Simply type your secret message into the text box.

  2. Encrypt and share: With a single click, your message is encrypted, and a unique One-Time Password (OTP) is generated.

  3. Unlock the secret: The recipient enters the OTP to reveal your message. Once viewed, the message is gone forever.

    4. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We don't store your messages, and we don't require you to create an account. Your conversations are your own, and at Whim, we intend to keep it that way.
